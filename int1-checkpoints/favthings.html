My Favorite Things

Cats

One of my favorite things are cats. Cats are the best animal because they are cute, easy to look after, and the best to hug.
I have one cat and she always meows loudly. She mostly stays indoors and sleeps everywhere. After a long day, picking
her up makes me happier. Also, all cats have adorable toe beans.

Volleyball

Volleyball is my favorite sport. Up until 7th grade, I hadn't been dedicated to any sport. Then, I was convinced to play on the school team that practiced at 6:30 in the morning, three times a week. Many of my friends had joined and soon we all spent time together passing the volleyball. Now, in 9th grade, I am on the high school team and practice everyday. I have games twice a week. All in all, volleyball is my favorite sport to play and my main source of exercise.

Dessert

Another favorite of mine is dessert. Dessert is possibly my favorite meal of the day; it is required for me to eat everyday to ensure happiness. There are so many different types: some with chocolate, some with fruit, some with different combinations.
